DocumenterDiagrams.jl

@diagram <format> block

Uses the Kroki.jl binding for kroki to generate diagrams in multiple formats.

```@diagram ditaa
+----------------+ 1  +---------------+  +------+
| @diagram block |--->| Documenter.jl |->| .svg |
+----------------+    +---------------+  +------+
                     2 |        ^      6
                       v      5 |  3
                      +----------+   +----------+
                      | Kroki.jl |-->| kroki.io |
                      +----------+   +----------+
                                ^     |
                                |  4  |
                                +-----+
```
